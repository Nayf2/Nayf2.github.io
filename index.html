Basil chicken

Serves: 6
Prep time: 20 mins
Total time: 60 mins
Category: Mediterranean
  • 1kg Chicken Thighs
  • 100g Basil
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Honey or Maple Syrup
  • 1 tbsp Rice Wine Viengar
  • 1 tsbp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tsp Mixed Italian Herbs
  1. In a blender combine all the ingredients together. Make sure the marinade has no unusual lumps ensuring it is smooth in consitency. Add more Salt and Pepper to taste to balance the flavours
  2. Start by cleaning the chicken thighs, being by using kitchen scissors to take off any skin and gristle on the chicken ensuring you pat each piece dry before placing in a container or ziplock bag
  3. Pour in the marinade ensuring all the chicken thighs are sufficiently covered in the marinade
  4. Leave the chicken to marinade for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight
  5. Fry the thighs over a medium heat so until around half way cooked ~ 5-10 mintues
  6. When the chicken is developing a nice golden colour transfer to a baking dish and cover with foil to ensure the chicken remains juicy
  7. Bake for an additonal 20-25 minutes or until the juice runs clear and serve straight away
  8. Enjoy with some homemade or shop bought chips as well as our low-calorie hummus dip which can be found below!
